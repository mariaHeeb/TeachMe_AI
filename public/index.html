The Challenge

Studying alone has its limits.

Many students prepare for exams by standing in their room pretending to teach an imaginary class. The method is sound — but without a real audience, it falls short.

🔇

No reactions

Nobody nods, looks confused, or signals whether your explanation actually landed.

❓

No questions

No one asks the follow-up that reveals the gap in your understanding.

😶

No feedback

You finish and have no idea if your explanation made sense — or where it broke down.

A classroom that listens back.

    About the App

    About TeachMe AI

    An interactive AI classroom that makes learning feel alive, engaging, and genuinely effective.

    TeachMe AI is an interactive AI classroom app designed to help people practice explaining what they know in a more active, engaging, and realistic way.

    💡 Why TeachMe AI?

    Many students prepare for exams by standing in their room and pretending to teach material to an imaginary class. Explaining a topic out loud genuinely strengthens understanding — but it quickly becomes monotonous when there are no real students, no questions, no reactions, and no one to point out what's unclear or incomplete.

    TeachMe AI was created to solve exactly this problem.

    The app gives users a virtual classroom of AI students who listen, react, raise their hands, and ask relevant questions based on the lesson. Instead of repeating information alone, users practice teaching in front of an interactive class — improving their understanding, confidence, and explanation skills with every session.
